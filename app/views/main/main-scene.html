Periodic Sync
If you only want to check how accurate your device is keeping time (without actually syncing), use "Periodic Sync" together with "Monitor Mode".
Periodic Sync
@Time Sync
You can schedule Clock Sync to sync at fixed times during a day. This feature can be used alone or mixed with "Periodic Sync". It also obeys the "Monitor Mode".
Monitor mode
In monitoring mode Clock Sync does NOT sync your device for real when triggered by the periodic sync or the fixed times. Instead it just monitors the time and drift and reports them to the history.
